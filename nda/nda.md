# NDA Acknowledgment (OIL)

## Overview

All members, contributors, and collaborators of Oasys Innovation Lab (OIL) must acknowledge and agree to this Non-Disclosure Agreement before gaining access to any OIL repositories or resources.

## Terms

By contributing to OIL repositories, you acknowledge and agree that:

1. **All work is Internal Use Only** unless explicitly approved for external release
2. **Confidential Information** includes all code, documentation, data, designs, processes, and communications within OIL repositories
3. **No Unauthorized Disclosure** - You will not share, publish, or disclose any OIL work outside the organization without written authorization
4. **No Personal Copies** - You will not fork private repositories to personal accounts or copy code to unauthorized locations
5. **Immediate Effect** - This agreement takes effect immediately upon acknowledgment and continues indefinitely
6. **Breach Consequences** - Violation of this NDA may result in:
   - Immediate revocation of all access
   - Disciplinary action up to and including termination
   - Legal action for damages

## External Release Process

If you believe work should be released externally (open source, publication, etc.):

1. Submit a formal release request to OIL leadership
2. Include justification and business case
3. Await written approval before any disclosure
4. Follow any conditions specified in the approval

## Acknowledgment

You acknowledge this NDA by:

- ✅ Checking the NDA acknowledgment box in your [Access Request](https://github.com/OasysInnovationLab/.github/issues/new?template=access-request.yml)
- ✅ Understanding that your access will be revoked if you violate these terms
- ✅ Agreeing to report any inadvertent disclosures immediately to oasysinnovationlab@oasysic.com
